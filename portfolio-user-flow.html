My Role

Go With The Flow & Take A Hike.

For this project, I was tasked with creating a user flow for an app Hike With Me was developing, a hike scheduling app. Their team had initially completed research and were able to share what they determined were their business goals and user personas they put together. It was important for them that the app flow in a logical order with the least amount of clicks a user needs to complete scheduling a hike.

I had to put myself in the shoes of the personas they provided and think about what they would click to complete their scheduling of a hike. To assist in keeping this mindset throughout the process I created a user flow/journey map as well as some sketches and wireframes. The result was a flow where small segments would be completed instead of filling out an entire form at once and that featured social interaction options.
